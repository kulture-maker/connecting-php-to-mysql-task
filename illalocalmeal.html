LOCAL HOTEL MENU

1.) UGALI BEAF

Price ksh 450

Ugali beaf is a common local food in kenya. Several tribes based in kenya take this meal during the evening or dinner as part of their three-course meal. Arizona local Hotel takes it to another level by providing this meal to its customers at its best taste.

2.)CHAPO AND NDENGU/BEANS

Ksh 120

Chapo Ndengu/beans is one other local meal served at Local Hotel Menu. Its considered a light-weight meal among sevel kenyans that is taken mostly during the daytime, that is, lunch breaks.

3.)CHAI AND MANDAZI

price Ksh 120

Chai which refers to tea is a morning meal to the local people that is taken everyday. The local hotel serves well cooked tea with mandazis.
